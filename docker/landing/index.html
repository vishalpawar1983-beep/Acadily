FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does it take to onboard my institute?
Most institutes are set up and running within 30 minutes. We create your tenant, configure branches, and import your courses — all while you're on a call with us.
Can I import data from my existing software?
Yes. We've already migrated institutes running on legacy Express apps, spreadsheets, and Google Sheets. If your data is structured, we can import it. If it's scattered, we'll help you clean it up.
Are my branches kept separate from other institutes?
Completely. Every document in Acadily carries a tenant identifier. Your students, fees, and reports are never visible to any other institute — not even to us without your explicit permission.
Do counsellors see all branches by default?
No. Role-based access controls which branches each person sees. Counsellors only see their assigned branch. Accounts staff see their company's ledger. You decide the permissions.
What happens if I outgrow my plan?
Upgrade anytime. Your data stays intact. Changes take effect immediately — no migration, no downtime, no data loss.
Who do I contact for support?
You talk directly to the team that built the platform. No tiered ticketing, no enterprise runaround — real answers from the people who know the code.
